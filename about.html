News app created with angular, foundation and webpack. It uses the following API (https://newsapi.org) to get data.
Search from over 70 news sources to get the top headlines.

Listed below are the app dependencies

angular: v1.6.2,
angular-ui-router: v0.4.2

babel-core: v6.23.1,
babel-loader: v6.4.0,
babel-preset-es2015: v6.22.0,

webpack: v2.2.1,
webpack-dev-server: v2.4.1
